Est. 2018

From a Humble Home Kitchen...

It all started in 2018, when our founder, Sarah, decided that "good enough" ice cream wasn't good enough. Armed with a single home-churning machine and a passion for local ingredients, she began experimenting with unusual flavor pairings.

What started as a hobby soon grew into a weekend pop-up, and eventually, our flagship store. Today, we're a team of dedicated "flavor architects" who believe that every scoop should bring a smile.
